About

Bridging biology and computation.

I'm a Research Scientist at Johnson & Johnson where I apply computational approaches to accelerate drug discovery and development. My work sits at the intersection of genomics, machine learning, and translational biology.

Before J&J, I completed my PhD in Computational Biology, focusing on single-cell multi-omics and gene regulatory networks. I'm passionate about turning large biological datasets into mechanistic insights that drive therapeutic hypotheses.
