About

Medicine as a calling.

I'm a junior pre-medical student with a deep passion for patient care, health equity, and translational medicine. I came to medicine through volunteering at community health clinics and witnessing first-hand how access to care transforms lives.

Outside the classroom, I conduct research in a immunology lab studying T-cell exhaustion in chronic disease. I'm also the co-founder of our campus Free Clinic and a certified EMT. I'm applying to medical school with the goal of practicing internal medicine in underserved communities.
